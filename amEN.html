ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Introduction

Abu Obaida Al-Jarrah Preparatory School for Boys was established in 1988 and named after the great Islamic leader. It opened as a morning scientific school and graduated many students who excelled academically and morally. Its graduates contributed to serving society in various fields.

The school was named after one of the great military leaders in the early days of Islam, the noble companion:

Abu Obaida Amir ibn Abdullah Al-JarrahMay Allah be pleased with him — Trustee of this Nation
One of the ten promised Paradise and among the first to embrace Islam. The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ called him "the Trustee of this Nation"

He was a prominent military leader in the Levant and one of the first to convert to Islam through Abu Bakr Al-Siddiq. He witnessed all major events alongside the Prophet ﷺ, including the famous incident where he removed arrows from the Prophet's face at the Battle of Uhud.

He led the Islamic armies in the Levant, achieving great victories, and played a major role in the Battle of Yarmouk against the Romans in 636 CE. He passed away from the plague in 639 CE at the age of 58 and was buried in the Jordan Valley.
